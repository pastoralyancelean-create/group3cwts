Busog na Bata, Masiglang Pamayanan

Feeding Program para sa Kabataang Katutubo

Community-based initiative · Conrazon, Bansud, Oriental Mindoro

Presented by: Khim Joethel Zaulda & NutriBo

Overview

The program is a community-based initiative addressing malnutrition among Indigenous Peoples (IP) children in Conrazon, Bansud.

Many IP children face limited access to nutritious food and health support, increasing vulnerability to long-term effects on growth and learning.

Overview — continued

The program provides nutritious meals to support physical and cognitive development, paired with nutrition education for parents and community members.

We emphasize inclusivity, cultural respect, and community participation to ensure the program meets local needs.

Goal

To reduce malnutrition among IP children by providing nutritious meals and promoting nutrition awareness, contributing to healthier and more active children.

Objectives

  • Provide nutritious meals to IP children at risk of malnutrition.
  • Improve health and energy through regular balanced intake.
  • Educate children and parents about proper nutrition.

Objectives — continued

  • Encourage community participation from students, teachers, and leaders.
  • Promote compassion, volunteerism, and social responsibility among volunteers.
